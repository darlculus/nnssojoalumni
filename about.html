About Our Association

The story of Nigerian Navy School, Ojo — and the brotherhood that has endured for over four decades.

Our Mission

A Brotherhood Forged in Discipline & Excellence

Nigerian Navy School, Ojo was established in 1982 to provide quality secondary education for children of Nigerian Navy personnel and the general public. The school produced its first set of alumni in 1988 — and that pioneer class laid the foundation for a legacy that has only grown stronger with each passing year.

The NNS Ojo Alumni Association exists to keep that bond alive — connecting old students across generations, supporting the school, and empowering our members to thrive.

Our History

Key Milestones

1982

School Founded

Nigerian Navy School, Ojo is established in Ojo, Lagos State, to provide quality education for children of naval personnel and the public.

1988

Pioneer Set Graduates

The first set of students completes their secondary education, marking the birth of the NNS Ojo alumni community.

1995

Alumni Association Formally Constituted

Old students formally establish the NNS Ojo Alumni Association with a constitution, elected executives, and a registered office.

2005

First Major Donation to School

The association donates a fully equipped science laboratory to NNS Ojo, the first of many infrastructure contributions to the school.

2015

Scholarship Fund Established

The NNS Ojo Alumni Scholarship Fund is launched, providing annual bursaries to outstanding current students from underprivileged backgrounds.

2023

UK & USA Diaspora Chapters Launched

International chapters are formally inaugurated in London and Houston, extending the association's reach to the global diaspora.

2026

This Website Launches

The official NNS Ojo Alumni digital platform goes live — connecting all sets, enabling dues payment, donations, and community engagement online.
