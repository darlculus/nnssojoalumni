FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as an alumni member? +
Click "Register" on the login page, fill in your details including your graduation set year, and submit. Your membership will be verified by the secretariat within 48 hours.
How much are the annual dues? +
The 2026 annual dues are ₦10,000. You can pay directly through your member portal using Paystack (card, bank transfer, or USSD).
I graduated but I'm not sure of my exact set year. What do I do? +
Contact the secretariat via email or phone with your approximate graduation year and full name. We'll help verify your set from our records.
Can I donate to a specific project rather than the general fund? +
Yes. In your portal, the Donate button lets you choose from the General Fund, Scholarship Fund, Library Project, or Welfare Fund — each linked to a separate association account.
How do I post a job on the job board? +
Visit the Job Board page and click "Post a Job". Job postings are free for verified alumni members and go live after a quick review by the admin team.
