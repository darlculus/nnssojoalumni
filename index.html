Our Story

A Community Forged in
Discipline & Excellence

Nigerian Navy School, Ojo was founded in 1982 and has been shaping young minds ever since. Our alumni — from the pioneer set of 1988 to the present — span the military, government, business, medicine, law, and the arts, united by the values instilled within those hallowed walls.

This platform exists to keep that bond alive — to reconnect old friends, celebrate achievements, support one another, and give back to the school that made us who we are.
