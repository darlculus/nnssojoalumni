Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

The NNS Ojo Alumni Association ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our members and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register or use our platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Graduation set year and school-related information
  • Current location, occupation, and employer
  • Payment information (processed securely via Paystack — we do not store card details)
  • Profile photo and biographical information you choose to provide
  • Usage data such as pages visited and features used

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your alumni membership account
  • To process dues payments and donations
  • To send you event invitations, newsletters, and association announcements
  • To display your profile in the alumni directory (subject to your privacy settings)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Paystack (our payment processor) — for processing dues and donations
  • Email service providers — for sending newsletters and notifications
  • Other alumni members — only the information you choose to make visible in your profile settings

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including SSL encryption, secure password hashing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nnsojoalumni.org.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

7. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@nnsojoalumni.org.
